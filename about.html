Our Story

RAGE FITNESS in Tagum City is a 24-hour fitness facility located on the 2nd Floor of the SUP Building at the Tagum Public Market, Barangay Magugpo West. A notable landmark near the gym is KANOFEE.

Facilities & Services

The gym offers a variety of equipment suitable for strength training, cardio workouts, and functional fitness. It caters to both beginners and seasoned athletes, providing a comprehensive environment for various fitness goals.

Operating Hours

RAGE FITNESS operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, accommodating members with varying schedules.
