What are your membership rates?

We offer various membership options to suit your needs:

  • Regular Rate: ₱1,000/month
  • Graveyard Rate (12AM-6AM): ₱500/month
  • Student Rate: ₱700/month (with valid ID)
  • Group Membership: ₱2,400/month (3+ members)

All memberships include 24/7 access to the gym, basic equipment usage, and locker room access.

Do you offer trial memberships?

Yes! We offer a one-day trial pass for ₱50. This allows you to experience our facilities and equipment before committing to a membership. Please bring a valid ID and appropriate workout attire.

What facilities do you have?

Our facility includes:

  • State-of-the-art cardio equipment
  • Complete range of strength training equipment
  • Boxing area with punching bags
  • Group class studio
  • Locker rooms with showers
  • Free parking area

Do you have parking available?

Yes, we have parking available in the public market area. The parking area is accessible 24/7 and is free for all members.

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of classes including:

  • Circuit Training
  • Boxing Fitness
  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
  • Strength Training Classes
  • Cardio Classes

Class schedules are available at the front desk and on our website.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we offer personal training sessions with our certified trainers. Our trainers can help you with:

  • Personalized workout plans
  • Nutrition guidance
  • Form correction
  • Goal setting and tracking

Contact us for personal training rates and availability.

Do I need to be experienced to join?

No experience is necessary! We welcome members of all fitness levels. Our trainers are available to help you get started and ensure you're using equipment safely and effectively. We also offer beginner-friendly classes and can create a personalized program to help you achieve your fitness goals.
